Useful Information You may Need in Istanbul
If you have any questions whether it is about Istanbul Airport Transfer or not, please feel free to contact us. We'll be pleased for helping foreigners as local people.
Some transfer companies requested my passport number based on the law. Do they rights?

Yes, It is correct. The law said that tourism companies must request name, surname, nationality and passport id of every passenger. (The point #36 describes it).

Can I get a Visa at Istanbul Airport?

Visa acquisition through the offices in the Istanbul Airport varies for each country citizen. In order not to have problems in the visa process, you can get preliminary information or you can apply electronic visa.

How can I get current flight information such as delay or cancellation of my flight?

What if I can't contact with you, What should I do?

Sometimes the worst scenario happens. Don't worry, Istanbul Airport provides 1 hour free WiFi for passengers. Connect and contact with us. You can obtain this service by entering your passport number or via SMS.

Where should I go if I have any problems in Istanbul?

Whether criminal events are not common in Istanbul, Istanbul Tourism Police will help you for any criminal case.

You can have options to contact tourism police by

  • WhatsApp:  +90 505 187 66 14
  • Email: istanbulturizm@egm.gov.tr
  • Email: info@istanbultourismpolice.com
